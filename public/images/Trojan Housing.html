Our Motivation


As a student at the University of Southern California, finding suitable accommodation can sometimes be challenging, particularly for non-local students new to the USC area, due to a lack of reliable information sources. Our goal is to extend a helping hand to every Trojan in search of a comfortable home while at school. Creating a seamless and enjoyable experience for all members of the Trojan community.
